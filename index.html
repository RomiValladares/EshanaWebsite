about Eshana

My name is Eshana, and massage has been part of my life for over 30 years. I began exploring the healing arts in 1989/90, following a deep curiosity about the body, energy, and the ways we hold and release experience. Over the years, I’ve studied a wide range of techniques with teachers from around the world, each one offering a new window into the art of healing through touch.

In 2003/4, I chose to formalize my training and became licensed in Santa Fe, New Mexico — a place that deeply shaped my connection to the spiritual and grounding aspects of bodywork. Since 2004, I’ve been honored to hold space for healing in the Hamptons, where I’ve built a practice rooted in presence, intuition, and trust.

At Eshana Massage Therapy, I offer Deep Tissue, Swedish, Pregnancy, Therapeutic, and Intuitive massage. My approach blends technical skill with intuitive listening, creating sessions that are fully personalized and guided by your body’s unique needs in the moment.

The name Eshana is not only my own — it also comes from Sanskrit, meaning desire, intention, or spiritual search. To me, it reflects the deeper purpose of this work: to support your return to balance, to stillness, and to the wholeness that’s always within you.
